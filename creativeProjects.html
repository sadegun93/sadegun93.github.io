For King and Country

An interactive narrative made in Twine. The player takes the role of an aide to the president of the United States.
Their goal is to help oversee the beginning of a proposed integration of earth and another, parallel world where magic is just a fact of life.
Or, if they so choose, to put a top to it before it gets underway.

Hope's Point

Decades after an alien invasion left earth largely decimated, a new settlement has formed in the ruins of Seattle, Washington.
Taking it's name from the Space Needle, the people hope to one day restore human civilization.
However, a constant stress for them are aliens who were stranded when their race abandoned earth, some of them more friendly than others, though there's no easy way to tell.

Harmonia

The kingdom of Harmonia is situated on an island. For centuries the Faust Dynasty has ruled the kingdom, guiding the people into the future.
The royal family, and all of the nobility, have a firm hold on their power and status because of their natural ability to use magic by communing with the spirits of nature.
They've amassed great wealth and large stores of resources with this power, forcing the rest of the population to rely on them.
The class divide is incredibly stark, but in recent years, magitech has become more and more common, allowing common born people to use magic themselves.
Opposition to this comes from across the political spectrum. Both because of how it upsets the status quo, and the effect it has on the spirits, and the rest of the environment as a whole.
